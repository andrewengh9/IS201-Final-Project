How It Works

Food entries increase your total consumed calories. Running and gym workouts increase total calories burned. The app updates your net calories as:

Net calories = consumed calories - burned calories

These are useful estimates, not medical advice. Real calorie needs and burn rates vary by fitness level, body composition, sex, workout efficiency, and intensity.
